Code of Conduct

Welcome to the AI Safety UAE Community!

We’re thrilled to have you! This community unites professionals, researchers, academics, technologists, policymakers, and curious minds in the UAE who are passionate about advancing AI safety.

Our Vision

AI Safety UAE exists to foster collaboration, dialogue, and innovation around safe and trustworthy AI. We are committed to creating an inclusive, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of gender, identity, ethnicity, faith, age, ability, or background. AI Safety UAE is committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment in any form.

Scope – Where and Who It Applies

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces and activities, including but not limited to:

  • Online platforms (Slack, LinkedIn, forums, social media groups)
  • In-person events (study groups, meetups, workshops, hackathons, conferences)
  • Virtual sessions (webinars, panel discussions, online meetups)
It is binding on all attendees, members, organisers, partners, and volunteers.

Our Standards: Expected Behaviour

Members are encouraged to:

  • Treat all participants with respect, courtesy, and professionalism.
  • Engage in discussions that are constructive, evidence-based, and inclusive.
  • Respect differing viewpoints, experiences, and expertise.
  • Give credit where it is due and respect intellectual contributions.
    • Follow event or platform-specific rules set by organisers or moderators.

Examples of expected behaviour:

  • Listening actively to others during discussions without interruption.
  • Offering constructive feedback (“I see your point, but what about X…”) instead of dismissive remarks.
  • Helping new members feel welcome by answering their questions.
